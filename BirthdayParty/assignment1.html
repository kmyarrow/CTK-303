"Angel of Mine" by Monica

What song was popular the week you were born?

I was born on Februray 25th, 1999. At the time, the song that was popular then was "Angel of Mine" by Monica. Her song is actaully a cover song. "Angel of Mine" was oringally performed by British R&B girl group Eternal from their first compilation album, Greatest Hits (1997). In 1998, Monica released a cover version which topped the US Billboard Hot 100 chart for four weeks and peaked at number two on the US Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart. It was certified platinum by the RIAA in 1999
